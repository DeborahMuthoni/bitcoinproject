FAQs(Frequently Asked Questions)

General

? What is Bitcoin?
Bitcoin is a consesus network that enables a new payment system and a completely digital money.It is the first decentralized peer-peer payment network that is powered by its users with no central authority or middlemen.From a user perspecive, Bitcoin is pretty much like cash for the internet. Bitcoin can also be seen as the most prominent triple entry bookkeeping system in existence.
? Who controls the Bitcoin network?
Nobody owns the Bitcoin network much like no one owns the technology behind email.Bitcoin is controlled by all Bitcoin users around the world.While developers are improving the software, they can't force a change in the Bitcoin protocol because all users are free to choose what software and version they use. In order to stay compatible with each other,all users need to use software complying with the same rules.Bitcoin can only work correctly with a complete consesus among all users.Therefore,all users and developers have a strong incentive to protect this consesus.
? How does Bitcoin work?
From a user perspective,Bitcoin is nothing more than a mobile app or computer program that provides a personal Bitcoin wallet and allows a user to send and receive bitcoins with them.
? Is Bitcoin really used by people?
Yes. There are a growing number of people and businesses using Bitcoin.
? How difficult is it to make a Bitcoin payment?
Bitcoin payments are easier to make than debit or credit card purchases, and can be received without a merchant account.

Account

? What happens if I lose my Bitcoins?
Unfortunately, since unique private keys are associated with indivisual Bitcoin wallets, if the keys are lost,there is ultimately no way to retrieve that key without a passcode seed or other retrieved system; and that key is required to spend those coins. However, most moders wallets, like Mycellium, have wallet and key backups that you can build prior to storing money.This will allow you to create a new private key so that you may restore your private key on a new wallet if lost.

? How long does it take to withdraw from Bitcoin?
You must have a balance of at least 0.001 bitcoin to make a withdrawal. You can withdraw up to $2,000 worth of bitcoin every 24hours and upto $5,000 within any 7-day period. Transfers to external wallets usually take around two hours but can take longer.
